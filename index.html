Frequently Asked Questions

How much does tree removal cost?

Cost depends on the size, location, and condition of the tree. We provide free on-site estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no hidden fees. Contact us today for your personalized quote.

Do you offer emergency tree service?

Yes! Tree Bandit is available 24/7 for emergency situations like storm damage or fallen trees threatening your property. We understand that tree emergencies can't wait — call us anytime and we'll respond quickly.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully insured to protect both our crew and your property on every job. Your peace of mind is our priority, and we maintain all necessary licenses and certifications to operate in East Texas.

How long does tree removal take?

Most residential trees can be removed in a single day. Larger or more complex jobs may take 2-3 days. We'll give you a detailed timeline during your free estimate so you know what to expect.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Van Zandt County, Rains County, Wood County, Hunt County, and surrounding areas across East Texas. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, please reach out and we'll be happy to let you know.
